1. PRODUCT DESCRIPTION
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
RunixOS is an operating system developed and maintained by Rovel Stars. It
includes a mix of open-source components under RSSL and other compatible
licenses, as well as proprietary system applications and utilities distributed
in binary form.

3. DISTRIBUTION & REDISTRIBUTION POLICY
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
3.1 Non-Commercial Redistribution.
     Users may download, use, and redistribute the open-source portions of
     RunixOS for personal, educational, or research purposes, including modified
     or forked versions, provided that:
     - All modifications are clearly documented;
     - Attribution to Rovel Stars is preserved; and
     - The product name and branding are not used in a way that misrepresents
       official endorsement.

3.2 Commercial and Enterprise Redistribution.
     Commercial redistribution of RunixOS or its Derivative Works is prohibited
     unless authorized under a Partner or Enterprise agreement with Rovel Stars.
     Bulk/Enterprise Consumers may install the official closed-source RunixOS
     on multiple owned devices after purchasing valid deployment licenses.

3.3 Partner Distributions.
     Partners and OEMs may preinstall or distribute RunixOS under written
     agreement. Partners must not modify or redistribute Rovel Stars’
     proprietary modules, except as approved through the official Partner
     Certification process.

5. PARTNERS AND OEM DISTRIBUTORS
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
5.1 Partners may introduce visual or functional customizations (“skins” or
     “variants”) with Rovel Stars’ written approval. Such modifications must
     maintain software quality and security standards set by Rovel Stars.
5.2 Partners must not modify official Rovel Stars system applications or
     services unless explicitly permitted.
5.3 Partners are issued signing keys for their update files. All Partner
     update packages must be submitted privately to Rovel Stars for integrity
     review and approval prior to release on their respective update channels.
5.4 Rovel Stars reserves the right to recall or disable updates that violate
     these policies or pose quality, legal, or security risks.
5.5 Partners are strictly prohibited from adding third-party advertisements,
     telemetry, or scripts that collect personal data without user consent.
5.6 Partners must not require constant internet connectivity for local system
     features without a justified technical reason.

──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6. PRIVACY, TELEMETRY, AND USER CONSENT
──────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────
6.1 RunixOS will never collect personal data or transmit identifiable
     information without explicit consent.
6.2 Any telemetry (performance, stability, or diagnostic data) is opt-in and
     can be fully disabled.
6.3 Partners and distributors are required to comply with all relevant data
     protection laws and this Addendum’s privacy rules.
